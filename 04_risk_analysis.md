# Risk Analysis

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|---------|------------|
| Input lag variance | High | Include latency calibration in settings |
| Scope creep (multiple assist types) | Medium | Lock scope post-Alpha review |
| Player backlash (skill dilution) | Medium | Make feature optional, communicate benefits clearly |
| Schedule slip | High | Buffer 1 week contingency before live deployment |
