# Cross-Discipline Collaboration

## Design
- Owns feature definition & player experience goals  
- Collaborates with Engineering to define assist window timing  

## Engineering
- Implements assist logic, data hooks, and toggle integration  
- Works closely with QA on timing precision  

## Art/UI
- Designs accessibility icons & visual indicators for assist mode  
- Delivers mockups to Engineering by Week 3  

## QA
- Develops regression tests for timing variance  
- Reports metrics from playtest builds  

## Producer
- Tracks sprint progress in Jira / Trello  
- Hosts standups, retrospectives, and milestone reviews  
- Communicates risks to stakeholders weekly
